About Object

The house is new, was built in 2019, total area of the house 154 m2,
on a land of 6813 m2.

Interior and exterior are natural, eco materials.

Exterior finish - natural stone, ventilated facade with elements of Siberian larch. Roof made of glued beam, covered with a metal tile (Sweden). Terraces with wrought iron fencing. Aluminum windows.

The interior decoration of the walls is euro-lining and natural stone, the floor is parquet. Conducted water (spring) and electricity. The bathrooms and the kitchen are tiled. Plumbing is installed, a staircase to the first floor, a wood and electric stove. Steam heating in reserve. The house is for sale with furniture and appliances.

Ground floor:

  • living room;
  • kitchen;
  • covered veranda with panoramic windows;
  • bathroom;
  • hallway;
  • large summer kitchen with a separate entrance.

First floor:

  • two bedrooms;
  • dressing room;
  • bathroom;
  • two open terraces;
  • corridor.

Land

The house stands on fertile land surrounded by conifers and a garden, fruit trees - plum, apple trees, cherries, sweet cherries, quinces. A mountain stream and a river flow along the border of the site. Its source of clean drinking water.

On the site there is another house with a total area of 31 m2, for reconstruction, the area of the house can be increased. Also there is an opportunity to rebuild to a house, a sauna, etc.

Location

The site is located near the highway with the main stream of tourist routes, while the low noise level from cars due to trees. There is an opportunity to put a hotel, restaurant, cafe, farm, mini-enterprise, organize eco-tourism (eco-village). Nearby are the Biogradska Gora National Park, the Belasica ski resort.
